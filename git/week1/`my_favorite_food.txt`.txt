How to make Garlic Penne Pasta:
One of the main reasons we love this dish is because it’s simple and quick! You can make it start to finish in about 20 minutes.
 That’s my kind of dinner recipe! Just follow these easy steps…

1 Cook pasta according to packaged directions.
2 Melt butter in a medium sauce pan, add garlic. Cook for 1 minute over medium heat.
3 add flour and cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly.
4 Add milk and broth, stirring constantly. Cook until sauce boils and thickens.
5 Add parsley and parmesan cheese. Add desired salt and pepper. Continue stirring until cheese has melted. 
Mix in pasta and Serve immediately.
